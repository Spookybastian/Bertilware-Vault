Bertilware Vault

A secure and open-source password manager

Why download the Vault?

enhanced_encryption

Military-Grade Encryption

All of your passwords are encrypted using the Advanced Encryption Standard, the only cipher available to the public that is approved by the NSA for securing the highest level of classified information.

sync_alt

Serverless Syncing

Unlike other password managers, your data is never sent to any cloud servers when syncing across devices, instead your information is exchanged directly and securely between your devices.

password

Strong Password Generation

Using the built-in password generator, you can create secure and unique passwords for all of your online accounts.

file_download

Import from Browsers

Easily import all of your existing passwords, no matter what browser you use.
